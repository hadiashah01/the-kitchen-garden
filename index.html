Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend reserving a table for weekend dinners and large groups to ensure availability.

Can you host large wedding events?

Yes, we have multiple venues capable of hosting everything from intimate engagements to grand wedding receptions for up to 500 guests.

Is there dedicated parking?

We provide secure, ample on-site parking for all our guests, including valet services during major events.

Are the family attractions open daily?

The Mini Golf and Aviary are open daily from 4:00 PM until 11:00 PM, perfect for evening family outings.
